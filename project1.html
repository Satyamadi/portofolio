Overview

Making of Web Design for Booking Villa Harum Sari Reatreat

01CONCEPT & DEVELOPMENT

I developed the concept for Harum Sari Retreat’s website with a focus on a natural and elegant look. The design uses nature-inspired colors, modern typography, and a clean layout to highlight the hero section, gallery, and contact information clearly.

The website was built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I implemented subtle animations, an interactive photo carousel, a contact form, and an integrated location map. The layout is fully responsive, ensuring a smooth experience across desktop and mobile devices.

02Challenges
& Outcome

One of the main challenges was large image sizes that affected loading speed. I solved this by optimizing and applying lazy load techniques. The result is a modern, lightweight website aligned with Harum Sari Retreat’s brand identity and well-received by the client.

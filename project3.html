01Booking App

This website design focuses on user experience and a clean visual layout. The goal is to help users easily find travel destinations, accommodations, and nearby recommendations. The layout is minimalist and well-structured, creating a smooth and intuitive navigation experience.

The hero section highlights an interactive search feature with filters for location, dates, and guests to engage users right away. Visual elements enhance the atmosphere of each destination, while the “Explore nearby” and “Recommend place” sections make it easier to discover ideas based on distance and category. Overall, this design emphasizes easy access to information, a modern interface, and an enjoyable exploration experience for users.

02Travel Book

The concept of Dubali Travel started from the idea of making trip planning simple and fast. I designed it to help users easily find and book hotels or travel packages with a clean and modern layout. The turquoise and white color palette was chosen to create a calm, trustworthy, and professional feel.

I focused on clear navigation and structured flow starting from search, then showing popular hotels and top destinations. The design was inspired by major booking platforms but made lighter and more user friendly. Every element is meant to guide users smoothly through their travel booking experience.

03Minimalist Portofolio

The idea for this portfolio website came from the need to create a clean and personal space to showcase creative work. I wanted the design to feel modern, simple, and professional, but still reflect an artistic vibe. The main concept is minimalism letting visuals speak louder than heavy design elements.

I used a lot of white space to keep the focus on the artwork and typography to create strong visual hierarchy. Each section is designed to guide visitors naturally, starting from who I am, what I do, to the projects I’ve worked on.

The overall layout is inspired by creative portfolio trends used by modern designers. I added soft color accents and subtle shapes to make the interface more dynamic. The goal was to make the website not just a gallery, but a reflection of my design identity clean, structured, and expressive.

Overview

Making Logo, Poster, Mockups, and Content Designs

01Logo

I develop logo concepts by combining simple, memorable visuals with meanings that reflect the brand’s identity. The process starts with research to understand the values and needs, then I translate them into symbolic forms that remain flexible across different media. I focus on balance, color harmony, and negative space to ensure clarity at any size. This approach makes each logo not only visually appealing but also effective in communicating the brand message.

02Poster

I create posters with a strong visual impact by combining bold typography, dynamic compositions, and expressive imagery. Each design reflects a modern and experimental style, where textures, colors, and layouts are used to build a sense of energy and movement. I focus on creating visuals that not only look striking but also communicate a certain mood or narrative. This approach allows the posters to feel fresh, artistic, and connected to contemporary culture.

03Fleas

This design showcases a social media content concept for a healthy food brand called Fleas. The visual style focuses on a fresh look with natural green tones and modern typography to highlight a clean and healthy image. Each post features menu items like Buddha Bowls and Sushi Bowls with clear pricing, neat layouts, and consistent organic graphic elements. The goal is to attract Instagram users with appetizing visuals that are easy to understand. The design also includes promotional elements like “Hot Sale” and “45% Discount” to boost engagement and sales.

04Barren

This project showcases a modern social media design concept for Barren, a premium fragrance brand. The visuals highlight the elegance and personality of each perfume through bold compositions, sophisticated typography, and rich color palettes that reflect each scent’s character. The layout blends luxury and minimalism, focusing on the product’s shape and texture to create a high-end look. Promotional elements like “45% Off” and phrases such as “Versatile & Daily” or “Indoor & Night” emphasize usability and exclusivity. The overall design aims to strengthen brand identity and attract attention through stylish, editorial-inspired visuals.
